Math - Bar Modeling

Grades: 3,4,5

Strand: steam

Session: week-4

Instructor: Meadowbrook Faculty

Students will explore the powerful math problem-solving tool, bar modeling from the beginning. Bar modeling is used extensively in the Singapore Math program and allows students in grades 3 - 5 to solve problems often found in Algebra texts not by assigning variables, but by drawing the math relationships using bars. Your child will be amazed at their ability to solve complex, multi-step problems just by drawing!

Rocketry: Explorers +

Grades: 3,4,5

Strand: creative-arts

Session: week-5

Instructor: Jonathan Schmid, Meadowbrook Faculty

Following the high demand from last summer, It's Rocket Science returns in a new and expanded Explorers+ format.
