Session: week-4

Instructor: Meadowbrook Faculty

Students will explore the powerful math problem-solving tool, bar modeling from the beginning. Bar modeling is used extensively in the Singapore Math program and allows students in grades 3 - 5 to solve problems often found in Algebra texts not by assigning variables, but by drawing the math relationships using bars.
